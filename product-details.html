About Premium Aged Cheddar

Crafted in small batches using traditional methods, our Premium Aged Cheddar is a testament to artisanal cheesemaking. Each wheel is carefully aged for 24 months in temperature and humidity-controlled environments to develop its distinctive flavor profile.

The milk for this exceptional cheese comes from grass-fed cows raised on sustainable farms in the lush pastures of Vermont. The farmers we partner with are committed to animal welfare and environmentally responsible practices.

Flavor Profile

This well-aged cheddar offers a complex flavor experience that evolves as you taste it:

  • Initially smooth with notes of butter and caramel
  • Develops into a rich, nutty middle
  • Finishes with a pleasant sharp tanginess
  • Subtle crystalline texture from protein crystals that form during aging

Serving Suggestions

This versatile cheddar shines in many culinary applications:

  • On a cheese board with fresh fruits, honey, and artisanal crackers
  • Melted into gourmet grilled cheese sandwiches or mac and cheese
  • Grated over soups or baked dishes
  • Paired with full-bodied red wines, craft beers, or apple cider

Storage

To maintain optimal flavor and texture, store your Premium Aged Cheddar wrapped in cheese paper or parchment paper in your refrigerator's cheese drawer (40-45°F). For best results, bring to room temperature 30-60 minutes before serving.

Behind the Cheese

Our master cheesemaker, James Wilson, has been perfecting his craft for over 30 years. His dedication to traditional methods and unwavering quality standards ensures that every wheel of our Premium Aged Cheddar meets the highest expectations.
